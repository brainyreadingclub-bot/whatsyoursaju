Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

SAJU ("we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We aim for the strongest reasonable interpretation of GDPR, CCPA, and Korean PIPA principles.

1. What we collect

  • Birth data you enter (date, time, gender, optional name) — used only to compute your reading. Processed in your browser; not transmitted to our servers unless you choose to use the AI conversation feature.
  • AI conversation messages — when you ask the AI a question, your question and the chart context are sent to our serverless function and to Anthropic (the AI provider) to generate a response. We do not associate these with any personal identifier.
  • Anonymous analytics — Google Analytics 4 collects standard pageview and event data (no birth data, no chat content). IP addresses are anonymized by Google.
  • Ad personalization data — Google AdSense may set cookies for ad personalization, subject to your browser's settings and Google's policies.

2. What we do not collect

  • We do not require an account, email, or password.
  • We do not store your birth data in any database after the reading is generated.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to third parties.
  • We do not use birth data for marketing or profiling.

3. Cookies

We use cookies set by Google Analytics (analytics) and Google AdSense (advertising). You can disable these in your browser settings or via tools like the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Disabling cookies does not affect your ability to use the reading tool.

4. Third-party processors

  • Anthropic (claude.ai) — processes AI chat messages. See Anthropic Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Analytics, AdSense) — processes analytics and ad data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the site and serverless functions. See Vercel Privacy Policy.

5. International data transfers

Because we are based in Korea but use US-based providers (Anthropic, Google, Vercel), data may be transferred internationally. We rely on standard contractual clauses and equivalent safeguards offered by these providers.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PIPA (Korea), you have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Because we do not store birth data, there is generally nothing to access or delete on our side. For analytics or ad-related data, please use Google's tools or contact us at ytyt8730@gmail.com.

7. Children

SAJU is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us so we can address it.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: ytyt8730@gmail.com
